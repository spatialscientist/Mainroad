Welcome to DAHRSE-Midlands! We are a community of Digital Arts and Humanities Research Software Engineers (DAHRSEs) within the Midlands in England.

Get involved

  • Future Events: We are planning targeted surveys and workshops. If you are interested in participating, please contact Dr. Godwin Yeboah.
  • Join Our Community: Join our Slack channel "#dahrse-midlands" in the RSE Society's Slack workspace and follow us on social media! Please see other social media channels on this page.
  • Add Your Profile to the Community Network Page: Please send your profile information to be added to the community network page currently being developed. The initial information being collected are as follows:
    • Name: Full name.
    • Role/Title (and scope): Your current role or title within the organization or project. Please indicate in brackets the scope of your role/title. For example, Senior Research Software Engineer (Arts and Humanities, Social Sciences).
    • Affiliation: The institution or organization you are associated with.
    • Expertise/Research Interests: A brief description of your areas of expertise or research interests.
    • Contact Information: Email address or other preferred contact method.
    • Social Media: Social media handles like X, Bluesky, Mastodon, LinkedIn, etc.
    The website will be altered in the future to allow direct contribution using Github or other available options.
